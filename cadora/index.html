03 · The AI layer

AI that removes work — not
just another chatbot.

Every workflow gets a quiet intelligence layer that predicts, matches, drafts, and flags — so your team acts on decisions instead of chasing data.

OPTIMISE

Smart rota & routing

AI builds conflict-free schedules and efficient travel routes from geography, visit length, and staff availability.

MATCH

Instant cover matching

When someone calls in sick, the system ranks and notifies qualified, available carers in seconds.

SUMMARISE

Care-note intelligence

Long visit notes become clean summaries and handover briefs, with condition changes surfaced automatically.

PREDICT

Compliance risk radar

Expiring DBS, training, and visas are forecast and escalated before they ever threaten an inspection.

DETECT

Missed-visit anomaly alerts

Unusual patterns — late arrivals, skipped tasks, drift from care plans — are flagged in real time.

FORECAST

Demand & capacity planning

Model upcoming demand and staffing needs so growth never outruns your workforce.
